Hello! My name is Rosie! I love digging into data and using it to discover alternative solutions and methods for optimizing products and team workflows. I have created new systems, roadmaps, and strategies that have increased sales revenue and productivity. I take pride in being a proactive learner, for example, a couple of years ago I taught myself SQL.

Outside of work, I serve as the co-founder of The #IAm Project, a scholarship and mentorship program committed to empowering women of color to succeed in STEM fields. When the weather permits you can find me outside training for another half-marathon or enjoying a nice day at the park with my dog.
